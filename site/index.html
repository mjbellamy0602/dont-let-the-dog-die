About the book

A story about bad people who want to be good.

When TV (Yes, that is her name) finds herself stuck in a purgatorial videogame, she gets annoyed. When she finds out it is because she is dead, and this is her punishment for her cruel actions in life, she gets angry. When she learns the plot might involve a dog dying, she gets violent.

Don't Let the Dog Die is a story about humor, second chances, gory boss battles, and figuring out how to control your own plotline.

About the author

MJ Bellamy

MJ Bellamy is a writer, dog lover, and potentially three sheep in a trench coat based in the United States. She has been writing stories since she was ten and has been owned by dogs for just as long. Don't Let the Dog Die is her debut novel.

Yes, the dog in the book is based on a real dog. No, she will not tell you if the dog makes it.
